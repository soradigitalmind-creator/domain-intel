Year: 2019

Primary subgenre: accounting

Secondary subgenres: business

Summary: Purpose This paper aims to explore organisational intentions to use Big Data and Big Data Analytics (BDA) in external auditing. This study conceptualises different contingent motivating factors based on prior literature and the views of auditors, business clients and regulators regarding the external auditing practices and BDA.
