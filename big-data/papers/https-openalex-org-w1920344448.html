Big data, bigger dilemmas: A critical review

Year: 2014

Primary subgenre: big data

Secondary subgenres: autonomydisciplineengineering ethics

Summary: The recent interest in Big Data has generated a broad range of new academic, corporate, and policy practices along with an evolving debate among its proponents, detractors, and skeptics. While the practices draw on a common set of tools, techniques, and technologies, most contributions to the debate come either from a particular disciplinary perspective or with a focus on a domain‐specific issue.
