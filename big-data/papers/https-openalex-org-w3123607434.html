Convex Optimization for Big Data: Scalable, randomized, and parallel algorithms for big data analytics

Year: 2014

Primary subgenre: big data

Secondary subgenres: algorithmanalytics

Summary: This article reviews recent advances in convex optimization algorithms for big data, which aim to reduce the computational, storage, and communications bottlenecks. We provide an overview of this emerging field, describe contemporary approximation techniques such as first-order methods and randomization for scalability, and survey the important role of parallel and distributed computation.
