Abstract
Diabetic nephropathy is one of the most important concerns in nephrology, as well as in medicine at large. Rapidly increasing rates of diabetes throughout the developed world represent an emerging epidemic with profound consequences. This epidemic is likely to drive previously unforeseen rates of vascular target organ complications.
