The immunology of the vermiform appendix: a review of the literature

Year: 2016  DOI ↗

Main topic: internal medicine

Abstract
This literature review assesses the current knowledge about the immunological aspects of the vermiform appendix in health and disease. An essential part of its immunological function is the interaction with the intestinal bacteria, a trait shown to be preserved during its evolution. The existence of the appendiceal biofilm in particular has proved to have a beneficial effect for the entire gut.
