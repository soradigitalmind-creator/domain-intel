Computational fluid dynamics modelling in cardiovascular medicine

Year: 2015

Primary subgenre: medicine

Secondary subgenres: computer scienceengineering

Summary: This paper reviews the methods, benefits and challenges associated with the adoption and translation of computational fluid dynamics (CFD) modelling within cardiovascular medicine. CFD, a specialist area of mathematics and a branch of fluid mechanics, is used routinely in a diverse range of safety-critical engineering systems, which increasingly is being applied to the cardiovascular system.
