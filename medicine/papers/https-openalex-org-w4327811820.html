Kidney fibrosis: from mechanisms to therapeutic medicines

Year: 2023

Primary subgenre: medicine

Secondary subgenres: biology

Summary: Chronic kidney disease (CKD) is estimated to affect 10-14% of global population. Kidney fibrosis, characterized by excessive extracellular matrix deposition leading to scarring, is a hallmark manifestation in different progressive CKD; However, at present no antifibrotic therapies against CKD exist. Kidney fibrosis is identified by tubule atrophy, interstitial chronic inflammation and fibrogenesis
