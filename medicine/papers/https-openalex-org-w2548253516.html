Plasma medicine—current state of research and medical application

Year: 2016

Primary subgenre: heme oxygenase-1 and carbon monoxide

Secondary subgenres: supramolecular self-assembly in material

Summary: Plasma medicine means the direct application of cold atmospheric plasma (CAP) on or in the human body for therapeutic purposes. Further, the field interacts strongly with results gained for biological decontamination.
