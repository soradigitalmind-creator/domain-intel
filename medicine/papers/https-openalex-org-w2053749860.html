Trends and Challenges of Traditional Medicine in Africa

Year: 2011

Primary subgenre: traditional medicine

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Prior to the introduction of cosmopolitan medicine, traditional medicine used to be the dominant medical system available to millions of people in Africa in both rural and urban communities. However, the arrival of the Europeans marked a significant turning point in the history of this age-long tradition and culture. This paper examines the trends and challenges of traditional medicine in Africa.
