TCMSP: a database of systems pharmacology for drug discovery from herbal medicines

Year: 2014

Primary subgenre: medicine

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: The particular strengths of TCMSP are the composition of the large number of herbal entries, and the ability to identify drug-target networks and drug-disease networks, which will help revealing the mechanisms of action of Chinese herbs, uncovering the nature of TCM theory and developing new herb-oriented drugs. TCMSP is freely available at http://sm.nwsuaf.edu.cn/lsp/tcmsp.php.
