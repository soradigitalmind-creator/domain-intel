Update on treatments for nonmotor symptoms of Parkinson's disease—an evidence‐based medicine review

Year: 2019

Primary subgenre: medicine

Secondary subgenres: disease

Summary: The evidence base for treating a range of nonmotor symptoms in PD has grown substantially in recent years. However, treatment options overall remain limited given the high prevalence and adverse impact of these disorders, so the development and testing of new treatments for nonmotor symptoms in PD remains a top priority. © 2019 International Parkinson and Movement Disorder Society.
