Year: 2011

Primary subgenre: evidence-based medicine

Summary: As the name suggests, evidence-based medicine is about finding evidence and using that evidence to make clinical decisions. A cornerstone of evidence-based medicine is the hierarchical system of classifying evidence. This hierarchy is known as the levels of evidence.
