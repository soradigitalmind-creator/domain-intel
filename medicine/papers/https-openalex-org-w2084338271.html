Optically stimulated luminescence (OSL) dosimetry in medicine

Year: 2008

Primary subgenre: advanced radiotherapy techniques

Secondary subgenres: computer sciencematerial sciencemedical physics

Summary: This paper reviews fundamental and practical aspects of optically stimulated luminescence (OSL) dosimetry pertaining to applications in medicine, having particularly in mind new researchers and medical physicists interested in gaining familiarity with the field. A basic phenomenological model for OSL is presented and the key processes affecting the outcome of an OSL measurement are discussed. Prac
