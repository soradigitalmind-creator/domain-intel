The Smartphone in Medicine: A Review of Current and Potential Use Among Physicians and Students

Year: 2012

Primary subgenre: medicine

Secondary subgenres: computer sciencemedical education

Summary: While the smartphone's role in medicine and education appears promising and exciting, more high-quality studies are needed to better understand the role it will have in this field. We recommend popular smartphone applications for physicians that are lacking in evidence and discuss future studies to support their use.
