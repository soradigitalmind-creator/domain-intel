25th Anniversary Article: Rational Design and Applications of Hydrogels in Regenerative Medicine

Year: 2013

Primary subgenre: supramolecular self-assembly in material

Secondary subgenres: inhalation and respiratory drug delivery

Summary: Hydrogels are hydrophilic polymer-based materials with high water content and physical characteristics that resemble the native extracellular matrix. Because of their remarkable properties, hydrogel systems are used for a wide range of biomedical applications, such as three-dimensional (3D) matrices for tissue engineering, drug-delivery vehicles, composite biomaterials, and as injectable fillers i
