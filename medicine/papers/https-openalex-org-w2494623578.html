Year: 2016

Primary subgenre: anxiety

Summary: In Germany, psychosomatic medicine is not a subspecialty of psychiatry, although it has clear connections in terms of shared models, methods, and overlapping care for patients. At some places, most prominently in Heidelberg and Tbingen, departments of psychosomatic medicine are part of overarching departments for internal medicine.
