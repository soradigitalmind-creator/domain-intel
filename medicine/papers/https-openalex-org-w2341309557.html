Transfusion Medicine — Blood Transfusion

Year: 1999

Primary subgenre: intensive care medicine

Secondary subgenres: medicine

Summary: Blood transfusion and blood conservation (techniques or strategies to avoid the need for blood) are complementary activities that constitute the clinical arena of transfusion medicine. Recent improvements in the safety of the blood supply and the increasing costs associated with transfusion therapies have led to a reevaluation of the clinical practices of blood transfusion and blood conservation.
