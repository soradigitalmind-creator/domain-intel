Terahertz Technology in Biology and Medicine

Year: 2004

Primary subgenre: laser

Secondary subgenres: material sciencephysicsbiology

Summary: Terahertz irradiation and sensing is being applied for the first time to a wide range of fields outside the traditional niches of space science, molecular line spectroscopy, and plasma diagnostics. This paper surveys some of the terahertz measurements and applications of interest in the biological and medical sciences.
