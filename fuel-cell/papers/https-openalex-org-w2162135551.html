Computational Fluid Dynamics Modeling of Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells

Year: 2000  DOI ↗

Main topic: fuel cells

Abstract
A transient, multi-dimensional model has been developed to simulate proton exchange membrane (PEM) fuel cells. The model accounts simultaneously for electrochemical kinetics, current distribution, hydrodynamics and multi-component transport. A single set of conservation equations valid for flow channels, gas-diffusion electrodes, catalyst layers and the membrane region are developed and numerically solved using a finite-volume-based computational fluid dynamics (CFD) technique.
