The Bioperl Toolkit: Perl Modules for the Life Sciences

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: genomics

Secondary subgenres: programming languagesoftware engineering

Summary: The Bioperl project is an international open-source collaboration of biologists, bioinformaticians, and computer scientists that has evolved over the past 7 yr into the most comprehensive library of Perl modules available for managing and manipulating life-science information. Bioperl provides an easy-to-use, stable, and consistent programming interface for bioinformatics application programmers.
