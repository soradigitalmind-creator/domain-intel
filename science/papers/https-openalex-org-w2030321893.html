Is science multicultural?: postcolonialisms, feminisms, and epistemologies

Year: 1998

Primary subgenre: history

Secondary subgenres: philosophycontextdiversity

Summary: Is Science Multicultural? explores what the last three decades of European/American, feminist, and postcolonial science and technology studies can learn from each other. Sandra Harding introduces and discusses an array of postcolonial science studies, and their implications for 'northern' science. All three science studies strains have developed in the context of post-World War II science and tech
