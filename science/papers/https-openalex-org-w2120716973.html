Ecological responses to altered flow regimes: a literature review to inform the science and management of environmental flows

Year: 2009

Primary subgenre: abundance

Secondary subgenres: ecologygeography

Summary: Summary 1. In an effort to develop quantitative relationships between various kinds of flow alteration and ecological responses, we reviewed 165 papers published over the last four decades, with a focus on more recent papers. Our aim was to determine if general relationships could be drawn from disparate case studies in the literature that might inform environmental flows science and management. 2
