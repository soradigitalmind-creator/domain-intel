The Electric Car: development and future of battery, hybrid and fuel-cell cars

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: electric vehicles and infrastructure

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This book covers the development of electric cars, from their early days, to new hybrid models in production. Most of the coverage is focused on the very latest technological issues faced by automotive engineers working on electric cars, as well as the key business factors vital for the successful transfer of electric cars into the mass market.
