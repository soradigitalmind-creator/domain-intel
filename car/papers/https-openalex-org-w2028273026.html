Differential Transactivation by Two Isoforms of the Orphan Nuclear Hormone Receptor CAR

Year: 1997

Primary subgenre: computational biology

Secondary subgenres: gene

Summary: We have identified a new murine orphan member of the nuclear hormone receptor superfamily, termed mCAR, that is closely related to the previously described human orphan MB67, referred to here as hCAR. Like hCAR, mCAR expression is highest in liver. In addition to the most abundant mCAR1 isoform, the mCAR gene expresses a truncated mCAR2 variant that is missing the C-terminal portion of the ligand
