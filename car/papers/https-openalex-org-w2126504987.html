Older people and transport: coping without a car

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: economic growth

Secondary subgenres: geographypublic transport

Summary: Much of the literature on ageing and transport has been concerned with older drivers, which underlines the importance of private transport in their everyday lives, but little has been written about how a lack of transport impacts on quality of life. A survey was commissioned by the Office for Senior Citizens of the New Zealand government, and face-to-face semi-structured interviews were conducted
