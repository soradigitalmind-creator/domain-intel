Anti-CD19 CAR T Cells in CNS Diffuse Large-B-Cell Lymphoma

Year: 2017

Primary subgenre: car-t cell therapy research

Secondary subgenres: biopsyinternal medicine

Summary: In this case, a woman with refractory diffuse large-B-cell lymphoma had a CNS recurrence that responded completely to anti-CD19 CAR T-cell therapy. After biopsy of a subsequent skin recurrence, the lesions cleared in association with an expansion of the CAR T cells in the blood.
