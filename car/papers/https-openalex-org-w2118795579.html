An Epi-Detected Coherent Anti-Stokes Raman Scattering (E-CARS) Microscope with High Spectral Resolution and High Sensitivity

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: train

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We report an epi-detected coherent anti-Stokes Raman scattering (E-CARS) microscope that uses two synchronized picosecond pulse trains. The microscope not only provides high spectral resolution but also significantly improves sensitivity for three-dimensional vibrational imaging of living cells.
