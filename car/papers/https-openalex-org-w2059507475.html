Measuring Service Quality in the Car Service Industry: Building and Testing an Instrument

Year: 1992

Primary subgenre: marketing

Secondary subgenres: qualityservice

Summary: Service quality is becoming an important issue for Dutch car service firms. In order to measure the service quality delivered develops an instrument based on the quality dimensions and the SERVQUAL questionnaire developed by Berry, Parasuraman and Zeithaml. Describes the building and testing of the instrument. The outcome of the research is that the instrument is easily applicable for Dutch garage
