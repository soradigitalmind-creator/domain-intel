A Traffic Equilibrium Model of Travel by Car and Public Transit Modes

Year: 1977

Primary subgenre: mathematical optimization

Secondary subgenres: mathematical modeltraffic flowmode

Summary: We develop an equilibrium model of travel by private car and one or more public transit modes. The salient features of the model are the clear distinction between the flow of vehicles and flow of transit passengers and the means of modeling the interaction that occurs between private cars and public transit vehicles that use the same road links of the network.
