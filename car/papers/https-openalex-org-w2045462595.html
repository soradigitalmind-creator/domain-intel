Year: 1990

Primary subgenre: scheme

Summary: An introduction to the Car-Parrinello molecular-dynamics method is given, written from the viewpoint of a molecular physicist. This scheme makes it possible to simulate atomic and molecular motion in cluster or bulk systems on an energy surface that is determined ‘on the fly’, by finding an adiabatic electronic state appropriate to the instantaneous nuclear positions.
