Claudin18.2-specific CAR T cells in gastrointestinal cancers: phase 1 trial interim results

Year: 2022

Primary subgenre: car-t cell therapy research

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Despite success in hematologic malignancies, the treatment landscape of chimeric antigen receptor (CAR) T cell therapy for solid tumors remains limited. Claudin18.2 (CLDN18.2)-redirected CAR T cells showed promising efficacy against gastric cancer (GC) in a preclinical study. Here we report the interim analysis results of an ongoing, open-label, single-arm, phase 1 clinical trial of CLDN18.2-targe
