Sustained B cell depletion by CD19-targeted CAR T cells is a highly effective treatment for murine lupus

Year: 2019

Primary subgenre: car-t cell therapy research

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: The failure of anti-CD20 antibody (Rituximab) as therapy for lupus may be attributed to the transient and incomplete B cell depletion achieved in clinical trials. Here, using an alternative approach, we report that complete and sustained CD19<sup>+</sup> B cell depletion is a highly effective therapy in lupus models.
