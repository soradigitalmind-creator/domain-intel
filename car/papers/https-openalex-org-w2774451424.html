Kinodynamic trajectory optimization and control for car-like robots

Year: 2017

Primary subgenre: mathematical optimization

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This paper presents a novel generic formulation of Timed-Elastic-Bands for efficient online motion planning of car-like robots. The planning problem is defined in terms of a finite-dimensional and sparse optimization problem subject to the robots kinodynamic constraints and obstacle avoidance.
