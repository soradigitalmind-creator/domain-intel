End-to-end learning for lane keeping of self-driving cars

Year: 2017

Primary subgenre: computer vision

Secondary subgenres: convolutional neural networkend-to-end principleautonomous vehicle technology and safetyself driving

Summary: Lane keeping is an important feature for self-driving cars. This paper presents an end-to-end learning approach to obtain the proper steering angle to maintain the car in the lane. The convolutional neural network (CNN) model takes raw image frames as input and outputs the steering angles accordingly.
