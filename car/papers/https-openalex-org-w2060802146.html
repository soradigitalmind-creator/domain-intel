Activation of nuclear receptor CAR ameliorates diabetes and fatty liver disease

Year: 2009

Primary subgenre: biochemistry

Secondary subgenres: geneinternal medicine

Summary: Constitutive androstane receptor CAR (NR1I3) has been identified as a central mediator of coordinate responses to xenobiotic and endobiotic stress. Here we use leptin-deficient mice (ob/ob) and ob/ob, CAR(-/-) double mutant mice to identify a metabolic role of CAR in type 2 diabetes. Activation of CAR significantly reduces serum glucose levels and improves glucose tolerance and insulin sensitivity
