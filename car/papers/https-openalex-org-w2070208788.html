The concerns about recurrence scale (CARS): A systematic measure of women’s fears about the possibility of breast cancer recurrence

Year: 2003

Primary subgenre: breast cancer

Secondary subgenres: internal medicine

Summary: The Concerns About Recurrence Scale (CARS) systematically assesses the extent and nature of women's fears about the possibility of breast cancer recurrence. In this study with 169 breast cancer survivors, scores on the CARS indicate moderate levels of fear about recurrence overall and demonstrate a range in levels of fear.
