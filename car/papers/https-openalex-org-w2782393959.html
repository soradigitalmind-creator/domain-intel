Year: 2018

Primary subgenre: autonomous vehicle technology and safety

Summary: This paper presents a hierarchical trajectory planning based on the integration of a sampling and an optimization method for urban autonomous driving. To manage a complex driving environment, the upper behavioral trajectory planner searches the macro-scale trajectory to determine the behavior of an autonomous car by using environment models, such as traffic control device and objects.
