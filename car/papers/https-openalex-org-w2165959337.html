Year: 2007

Summary: We carried out a car-to-infrastructure (C2I) and car-to-car (C2C) 4x4 multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) radio channel measurement campaign at 5.2GHz in Lund, Sweden. This paper presents first results on pathloss, power-delay profiles, and delay-Doppler spectra in a C2C highway scenario, where both cars were traveling in opposite directions.
