Car plate recognition by neural networks and image processing

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: vehicle license plate recognition

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: In this paper we describe an experimental system for the recognition of Italian-style car license plates. Images are usually taken from a camera at a toll gate and preprocessed by a fast and robust 1-D DFT scheme to find the plate and character positions.
