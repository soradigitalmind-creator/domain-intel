Performance analysis of a car-to-car visible light communication system

Year: 2015

Primary subgenre: communications system

Secondary subgenres: aerospace engineeringrangefloating car data

Summary: This paper presents an analytical performance analysis of a car-to-car visible light communications system under different communication geometries during the daytime. A market-weighted headlamp beam-pattern model, measured dirt effects on light distribution, and the road-surface reflection model are employed.
