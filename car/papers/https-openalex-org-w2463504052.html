A 3-kW Wireless Power Transfer System for Sightseeing Car Supercapacitor Charge

Year: 2016

Primary subgenre: capacitance

Secondary subgenres: electrical engineeringpower

Summary: Wireless power transfer (WPT) is the preferred charging method for electric vehicles (EVs) powered by battery and supercapacitor. In this paper, a novel WPT system with constant current charging capability for sightseeing car with supercapacitor storage is designed. First, an optimized magnetic coupler using ferrite cores and magnetic shielding structure is proposed to ensure stable power transfer
