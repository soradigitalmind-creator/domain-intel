Year: 2011

Summary: Abstract The Car–Parrinello (CP) method made molecular dynamics simulation with on‐the‐fly computation of interaction potentials from electronic structure theory computationally feasible. The method reformulates ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD) as a two‐component classical dynamical system. This approach proved to be valuable far beyond the original CP molecular dynamics method.
