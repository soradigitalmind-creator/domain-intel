The Role of NO and NO<sub>2</sub> in the Chemistry of the Troposphere and Stratosphere

Year: 1979

Main topic: process

Related topics:

Summary: Oxides of nitrogen play an important role in the radical chemistry of the atmosphere and in the production and destruction of tropospheric and stratospheric ozone. Ozone is a principal agent in forming the OH radical which attacks inert gases in the troposphere. The acidity of precipitation is in part the result of HNO3 and H2SO4 formed by reactions involving the OH radical. Fast removal processes
