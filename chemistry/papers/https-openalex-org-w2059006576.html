Tropospheric chemistry: A global perspective

Year: 1981

Main topic: environmental organic chemistry

Related topics:

Summary: A model for the photochemistry of the global troposphere constrained by observed concentrations of H 2 O, O 3 , CO, CH 4 , NO, NO 2 , and HNO 3 is presented. Data for NO and NO 2 are insufficient to define the global distribution of these gases but are nonetheless useful in limiting several of the more uncertain parameters of the model.
