A new mechanism for regional atmospheric chemistry modeling

Year: 1997

Main topic: handbook of chemistry

Related topics:

Summary: A new gas‐phase chemical mechanism for the modeling of regional atmospheric chemistry, the “Regional Atmospheric Chemistry Mechanism” (RACM) is presented. The mechanism is intended to be valid for remote to polluted conditions and from the Earth's surface through the upper troposphere.
