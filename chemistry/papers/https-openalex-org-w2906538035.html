Quantum Chemistry in the Age of Quantum Computing

Year: 2019

Main topic: handbook of chemistry

Related topics:

Summary: Practical challenges in simulating quantum systems on classical computers have been widely recognized in the quantum physics and quantum chemistry communities over the past century. Although many approximation methods have been introduced, the complexity of quantum mechanics remains hard to appease. The advent of quantum computation brings new pathways to navigate this challenging and complex land
