Halogen Bonding: A Paradigm in Supramolecular Chemistry

Year: 2001

Main topic: handbook of chemistry

Summary: The term halogen bonding describes the tendency of halogen atoms to interact with lone pair possessing atoms. The binding features and structural properties of halogen bonding are discussed and applied to drive the intermolecular self-assembly of hydrocarbons and perfluorocarbons in chemo-, site-, and enantioselective supramolecular synthesis.
