The NIST Chemistry WebBook:  A Chemical Data Resource on the Internet

Year: 2001

Main topic: handbook of chemistry

Related topics:

Summary: The NIST Chemistry WebBook (http://webbook.nist.gov) is an Internet site that provides access to chemical and physical property data both from NIST and other sources. The site was established in 1996 and has grown to encompass a wide variety of thermochemical, ion energetics, solubility, and spectroscopic data.
