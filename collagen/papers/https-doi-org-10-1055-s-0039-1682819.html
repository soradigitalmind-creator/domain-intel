Aspirin, Collagen and the Collagen-Platelet Interaction

Year: 1977  DOI ↗

Main topic: —

Abstract
While the effect of aspirin (ASA) on platelets has been investigated by many laboratories, the effect of ASA on collagen in collagen-initiated platelet aggregation has not. We report here on the rate of collagen fibril formation in the presence of ASA from 0.1 to 15 mM, and on the resultant perturbation of platelet aggregation by such ASA-treated collagen.
