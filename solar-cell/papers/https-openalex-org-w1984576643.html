Engineering of Efficient Panchromatic Sensitizers for Nanocrystalline TiO<sub>2</sub>-Based Solar Cells

Year: 2001  DOI ↗

Main topic: dye-sensitized solar cell

Abstract
A new series of panchromatic ruthenium(II) sensitizers derived from carboxylated terpyridyl complexes of tris-thiocyanato Ru(II) have been developed.
