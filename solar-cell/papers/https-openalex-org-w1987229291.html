Device annealing effect in organic solar cells with blends of regioregular poly(3-hexylthiophene) and soluble fullerene

Year: 2005  DOI ↗

Abstract
Here we report enhanced efficiency bulk heterojunction organic solar cells using blend films of regioregular poly(3-hexylthiophene) (P3HT) and 1-(3-methoxycarbonyl)-propyl-1-phenyl-(6,6)C61 (PCBM) that are subjected to a thermal annealing process. Blend films (P3HT:PCBM=1:1 by weight) were prepared using chlorobenzene and 1,2-dichlorobenzene in order to investigate the role of the solvent.
