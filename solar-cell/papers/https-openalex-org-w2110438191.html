Molecular Design of Coumarin Dyes for Efficient Dye-Sensitized Solar Cells

Year: 2002  DOI ↗

Main topic: dye-sensitized solar cell

Abstract
We have developed novel coumarin dyes for use in dye-sensitized nanocrystalline TiO2 solar cells (DSSCs). The absorption spectra of these novel coumarin dyes are red-shifted remarkably in the visible region relative to the spectrum of C343, a conventional coumarin dye. Introduction of a methine unit (−CHCH−) connecting both the cyano (−CN) and carboxyl (−COOH) groups into the coumarin framework expanded the π conjugation in the dye and thus resulted in a wide absorption in the visible region.
