Daily Diary Designs in Lifespan Developmental Psychology

Year: 2018

Main topic: history of psychology

Related topics:

Summary: Daily diary designs allow researchers to examine processes that change together on a daily basis, often in a naturalistic setting. By studying within-person covariation between daily processes, one can more precisely establish the short-term effects and temporal ordering of concrete daily experiences.
