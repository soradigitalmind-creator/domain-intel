Direct detectors and their applications in electron microscopy for materials science

Year: 2021  DOI ↗

Abstract
Abstract The past decade has seen rapid advances in direct detector technology for electron microscopy. Direct detectors are now having an impact on a number of techniques in transmission electron microscopy (TEM), scanning electron microscopy, and scanning TEM (STEM), including single particle cryogenic electron microscopy, in situ TEM, electron backscatter diffraction, four-dimensional STEM, and electron energy loss spectroscopy.
