Year: 2018

Primary subgenre: lithium-ion battery

Summary: Abstract The use of solid electrolytes is a promising direction to improve the energy density of lithium‐ion batteries. However, the low ionic conductivity of many solid electrolytes currently hinders the performance of solid‐state batteries.
