Dynamic lithium-ion battery model for system simulation

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: advanced battery technologies research

Secondary subgenres: advancements in battery materiallithium-ion batterylithium-ion batterylithium-ion battery

Summary: Presents here a complete dynamic model of a lithium ion battery that is suitable for virtual-prototyping of portable battery-powered systems. The model accounts for nonlinear equilibrium potentials, rate- and temperature-dependencies, thermal effects and response to transient power demand. The model is based on publicly available data such as the manufacturers' data sheets. The Sony US18650 is use
