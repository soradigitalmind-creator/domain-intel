Phosphorus-Based Alloy Materials for Advanced Potassium-Ion Battery Anode

Year: 2017

Main topic: assessment battery children

Related topics:

Summary: Potassium-ion batteries (PIBs) are interesting as one of the alternative metal-ion battery systems to lithium-ion batteries (LIBs) due to the abundance and low cost of potassium. We have herein investigated Sn4P3/C composite as a novel anode material for PIBs. The electrode delivered a reversible capacity of 384.8 mA h g–1 at 50 mA g–1 and a good rate capability of 221.9 mA h g–1, even at 1 A g–1.
