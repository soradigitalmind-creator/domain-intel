Graphene-Wrapped Fe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>4</sub> Anode Material with Improved Reversible Capacity and Cyclic Stability for Lithium Ion Batteries

Year: 2010

Primary subgenre: advancements in battery material

Secondary subgenres: composite material

Summary: A well-organized flexible interleaved composite of graphene nanosheets (GNSs) decorated with Fe3O4 particles was synthesized through in situ reduction of iron hydroxide between GNSs.
