Polysulfide Shuttle Study in the Li/S Battery System

Year: 2004

Main topic:

Summary: This work reports a quantitative analysis of the shuttle phenomenon in Li/S rechargeable batteries. The work encompasses theoretical models of the charge process, charge and discharge capacity, overcharge protection, thermal effects, self-discharge, and a comparison of simulated and experimental data.
