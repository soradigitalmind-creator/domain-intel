Semi‐Solid Lithium Rechargeable Flow Battery

Year: 2011

Primary subgenre: advancements in battery material

Secondary subgenres: battery

Summary: A new kind of flow battery is fueled by semi-solid suspensions of high-energy-density lithium storage compounds that are electrically ‘wired’ by dilute percolating networks of nanoscale conductor particles. Energy densities are an order of magnitude greater than previous flow batteries; new applications in transportation and grid-scale storage may result. Detailed facts of importance to specialist
