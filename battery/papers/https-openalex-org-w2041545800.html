Optimizing for Efficiency or Battery Life in a Battery/Supercapacitor Electric Vehicle

Year: 2012

Primary subgenre: advanced battery technologies research

Secondary subgenres: automotive engineeringpower

Summary: A novel energy control strategy for a battery/supercapacitor vehicle, which is designed to be tunable to achieve different goals, is described. Two possible goals for adding a pack of supercapacitors are examined for a test vehicle using lead-acid batteries: 1) improving the vehicle's efficiency and range and 2) reducing the peak currents in the battery pack to increase battery life.
