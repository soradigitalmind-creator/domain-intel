High Capacity Li Ion Battery Anodes Using Ge Nanowires

Year: 2007

Primary subgenre: advancements in battery material

Secondary subgenres: physical chemistryion

Summary: Ge nanowire electrodes fabricated by using vapor-liquid-solid growth on metallic current collector substrates were found to have good performance during cycling with Li. An initial discharge capacity of 1141 mA.h/g was found to be stable over 20 cycles at the C/20 rate. High power rates were also observed up to 2C with Coulombic efficiency > 99%.
