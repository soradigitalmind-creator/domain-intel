Methodology for optimally sizing the combination of a battery bank and PV array in a wind/PV hybrid system

Year: 1996

Primary subgenre: automotive engineering

Secondary subgenres: computer scienceelectrical engineering

Summary: In this paper, a methodology for calculation of the optimum size of a battery bank and the PV array for a standalone hybrid wind/PV power system is developed. Long term data of wind speed and irradiance recorded for every hour of the day for 30 years were used. These data were used to calculate the average power generated by a wind turbine and a PV module for every hour of a typical day in a month
