Year: 2019

Main topic: assessment battery children

Summary: Graphite as an anode for the potassium ion battery (PIBs) has the merits of low cost and potentially high energy density, while suffering from limited cycle time and inferior stability. Herein we, using a concentrated electrolyte, demonstrate that formation of a robust inorganic-rich passivation layer on the graphite anode could resolve these problems.
