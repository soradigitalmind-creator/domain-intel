Unlocking the Potential of Cation-Disordered Oxides for Rechargeable Lithium Batteries

Year: 2014

Primary subgenre: lithium battery

Secondary subgenres: lithium

Summary: Nearly all high-energy density cathodes for rechargeable lithium batteries are well-ordered materials in which lithium and other cations occupy distinct sites. Cation-disordered materials are generally disregarded as cathodes because lithium diffusion tends to be limited by their structures. The performance of Li1.211Mo0.467Cr0.3O2 shows that lithium diffusion can be facile in disordered materials
