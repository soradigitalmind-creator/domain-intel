Life Cycle Testing of Electric Vehicle Battery Modules

Year: None

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This SAE Recommended Practice defines a standardized test method to determine the expected service life, in cycles, of electric vehicle battery modules. It is based on a set of nominal or baseline operating conditions in order to characterize the expected degradation in electrical performance as a function of life and to identify relevant failure mechanisms where possible.
