A New Approach to Quantum-Statistical Mechanics

Year: 1955

Primary subgenre: statistical mechanics

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A new method of calculating the grand partition function of many-body system is developed, adopting extensively the techniques of calculus in quantum field theory. It is shown that the grand partition function, which is a trace of the density matrix expressed in terms of field operators, can be evaluated in a way almost parallel with the evaluation of the vacuum expectation value of the S-matrix i
