Adjustment of Muscle Mechanics Model Parameters to Simulate Dynamic Contractions in Older Adults

Year: 2003

Main topic: statistical mechanics

Related topics:

Summary: The generation of muscle-actuated simulations that accurately represent the movement of old adults requires a model that accounts for changes in muscle properties that occur with aging. An objective of this study was to adjust the parameters of Hill-type musculo-tendon models to reflect nominal age-related changes in muscle mechanics that have been reported in the literature.
