Fractional quantum mechanics

Year: 2000

Primary subgenre: classical mechanics

Secondary subgenres: statistical mechanics

Summary: A path integral approach to quantum physics has been developed. Fractional path integrals over the paths of the Levy flights are defined. It is shown that if the fractality of the Brownian trajectories leads to standard quantum and statistical mechanics, then the fractality of the Levy paths leads to fractional quantum mechanics and fractional statistical mechanics.
