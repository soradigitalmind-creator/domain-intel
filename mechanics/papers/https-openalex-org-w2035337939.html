Mechanics of rollable and foldable film-on-foil electronics

Year: 1999

Main topic: statistical mechanics

Related topics:

Summary: The mechanics of film-on-foil devices is presented in the context of thin-film transistors on steel and plastic foils. Provided the substrates are thin, such transistors function well after the foils are rolled to small radii of curvature.
