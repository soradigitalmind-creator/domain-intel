Supersymmetry in Quantum Mechanics

Year: 2001

Main topic: statistical mechanics

Related topics:

Summary: An elementary introduction is given to the subject of supersymmetry in quantum mechanics which can be understood and appreciated by any one who has taken a first course in quantum mechanics. We demonstrate with explicit examples that given a solvable problem in quantum mechanics with n bound states, one can construct n new exactly solvable Hamiltonians having n − 1, n − 2,…, 0 bound states.
