Statistical mechanics of cellular automata

Year: 1983

Primary subgenre: mathematical analysis

Secondary subgenres: algorithmstatistical physics

Summary: Cellular automata are used as simple mathematical models to investigate self-organization in statistical mechanics. A detailed analysis is given of "elementary" cellular automata consisting of a sequence of sites with values 0 or 1 on a line, with each site evolving deterministically in discrete time steps according to definite rules involving the values of its nearest neighbors.
