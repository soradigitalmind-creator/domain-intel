Statistical Mechanics of Dimers on a Plane Lattice

Year: 1961

Primary subgenre: statistical mechanics

Secondary subgenres: combinatorics

Summary: This paper considers the statistical mechanics of hard rigid dimers distributed on a lattice (each dimer occupying two nearest neighbor lattice sites). The problem is solved in exact closed form for a finite $m\ifmmode\times\else\texttimes\fi{}n$ plane square lattice with edges which is completely filled with $\frac{1}{2}\mathrm{mn}$ dimers (close-packed limit).
