A Continuous Damage Mechanics Model for Ductile Fracture

Year: 1985

Main topic: contact mechanics

Related topics: fracture mechanicsfluid mechanicslagrangian mechanicsapplied mechanics

Summary: A model of isotropic ductile plastic damage based on a continuum damage variable, on the effective stress concept and on thermodynamics is derived. The damage is linear with equivalent strain and shows a large influence of triaxiality by means of a damage equivalent stress.
