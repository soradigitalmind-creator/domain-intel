Contact Mechanics

Year: 1985

Primary subgenre: classical mechanics

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This treatise is concerned with the stresses and deformation of solid bodies in contact with each other, along curved surfaces which touch initially at a point or along a line. Examples are a railway wheel and rail, or a pair of gear wheel teeth.
