Fracture mechanics and the adherence of viscoelastic bodies

Year: 1978

Primary subgenre: fracture mechanics

Secondary subgenres: adhesion, friction, and surface interactionscomposite materialcontact mechanicsfinite element method

Summary: The strain energy release rate G and its derivative delta G/ delta A are used to provide a general picture of the adherence of viscoelastic bodies. Two bodies in contact on an area A are in equilibrium if G=w, where w is the thermodynamic (or Dupre's) work of adhesion.
