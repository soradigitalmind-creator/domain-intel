A Model for the Mechanics of Jointed Rock

Year: 1968

Primary subgenre: tunneling and rock mechanics

Secondary subgenres: classification of discontinuities

Summary: The representation of discontinuities in analysis of blocky rock is discussed. A linkage type element is developed for addition of rock joint stiffness to the structural stiffness matrix describing the behavior of a system of rock blocks and joints.
