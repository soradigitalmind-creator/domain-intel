Column buckling of multiwalled carbon nanotubes using nonlocal continuum mechanics

Year: 2003

Primary subgenre: classical mechanics

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A model, based on the theory of nonlocal continuum mechanics, on the column buckling of multiwalled carbon nanotubes is presented. The present analysis considers that each of the nested concentric tubes is an individual column and that the deflection of all the columns is coupled together through the van der Waals interactions between adjacent tubes.
