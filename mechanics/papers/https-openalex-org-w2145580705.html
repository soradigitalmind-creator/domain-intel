On the use of isoparametric finite elements in linear fracture mechanics

Year: 1976

Primary subgenre: fatigue and fracture mechanics

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Abstract Quadratic isoparametric elements which embody the inverse square root singularity are used in the calculation of stress intensity factors of elastic fracture mechanics. Examples of the plane eight noded isoparametric element show that it has the same singularity as other special crack tip elements, and still includes the constant strain and rigid body motion modes.
