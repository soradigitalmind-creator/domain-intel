The mechanics of two-dimensional cellular materials

Year: 1982

Main topic: statistical mechanics

Related topics:

Summary: Abstract The mechanical properties (linear and nonlinear elastic and plastic) of two-dimensional cellular materials, or honeycombs, are analysed and compared with experiments. The properties are well described in terms of the bending, elastic buckling and plastic collapse of the beams that make up the cell walls.
