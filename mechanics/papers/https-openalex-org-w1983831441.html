Sculptured thin films and glancing angle deposition: Growth mechanics and applications

Year: 1997  DOI ↗

Main topic: —

Abstract
Sculptured thin films with three dimensional microstructure controlled on the 10 nm scale were fabricated with an evaporation technique. Glancing angle deposition (GLAD) and substrate motion were employed to “sculpt” columnar thin film microstructure into desired forms ranging from zigzag shaped to helical to four-sided “square” helical.
