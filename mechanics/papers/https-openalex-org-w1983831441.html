Year: 1997

Primary subgenre: classical mechanics

Summary: Sculptured thin films with three dimensional microstructure controlled on the 10 nm scale were fabricated with an evaporation technique. Glancing angle deposition (GLAD) and substrate motion were employed to “sculpt” columnar thin film microstructure into desired forms ranging from zigzag shaped to helical to four-sided “square” helical.
