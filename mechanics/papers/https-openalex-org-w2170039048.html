Discrete mechanics and variational integrators

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: mathematical analysis

Secondary subgenres: dissipation

Summary: This paper gives a review of integration algorithms for finite dimensional mechanical systems that are based on discrete variational principles. The variational technique gives a unified treatment of many symplectic schemes, including those of higher order, as well as a natural treatment of the discrete Noether theorem.
