Mechanics of Fiber Reinforcement in Sand

Year: 1983

Primary subgenre: composite material

Secondary subgenres: material sciencemechanicsapplied mechanics

Summary: Direct shear tests were run on a dry sand reinforced with different types of fibers. Both natural and synthetic fibers plus metal wires were tested. Experimental behavior was compared with theoretical predictions based on a force equilibrium model of a fiber reinforced sand.
