Year: 1930

Primary subgenre: classical mechanics

Secondary subgenres: geotechnical engineering and soil mechanicsstatistical mechanics

Summary: The old theory that frost heaving is due to change in volume of water frozen was based on experiments with closed systems. Field observations and recent experiments indicate that soils, when subjected to freezing under normal conditions, usually behave as open systems.
