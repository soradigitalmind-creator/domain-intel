Year: 2013

Main topic: classical mechanics

Abstract
We present geometric derivations of the Smarr formula for static AdS black holes and an expanded first law that includes variations in the cosmological constant. These two results are further related by a scaling argument based on Euler’s theorem. The key new ingredient in the constructions is a two-form potential for the static Killing field.
