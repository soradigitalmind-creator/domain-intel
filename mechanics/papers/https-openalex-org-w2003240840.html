Ligand Binding: Molecular Mechanics Calculation of the Streptavidin-Biotin Rupture Force

Year: 1996

Main topic: statistical mechanics

Related topics:

Summary: The force required to rupture the streptavidin-biotin complex was calculated here by computer simulations. The computed force agrees well with that obtained by recent single molecule atomic force microscope experiments.
