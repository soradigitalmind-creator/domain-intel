The Philosophy of the Limit

Year: 2016

Primary subgenre: philosophy of law

Secondary subgenres: economic justicelaw

Summary: In The Philosophy of the Limit Drucilla Cornell examines the relationship of deconstruction to questions of ethics, justice and legal interpretation. She argues that renaming deconstruction "the philosophy of the limit" will allow us to be more precise about what deconstruction actually is philosophically and hence to articulate more clearly its significance for law.
