Year: 1989

Primary subgenre: law

Secondary subgenres: law in society and culturepolitical science

Summary: In pre-revolutionary Russia, law was widely criticized - in the name of Christ, or in the name of Marx. Professor Walicki opens his study with an analysis of this tradition of hostility to law.
