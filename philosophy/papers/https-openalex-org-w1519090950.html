Reason in philosophy: animating ideas

Year: 2010

Primary subgenre: philosophical ethics and theory

Secondary subgenres: philosophical theory

Summary: Transcendentalism never came to an end in America. It just went underground for a stretch, but is back in full force in Robert Brandom's new book. An emphasis on our capacity to reason, rather than merely to represent, has been growing in philosophy over the last thirty years, and Robert Brandom has been at the center of this development. This is a paradigmatic work of contemporary philosophy.
