Year: 1982

Primary subgenre: democracy

Secondary subgenres: lawlaw in society and culturepolitical science

Summary: This book is both an exposition and a critical assessment of some central issues in jurisprudence and political theory. Chapter themes include Bentham's identification of the forms of mystification protecting the law from criticism, his relation to Beccaria and his conversion to democratic radicalism.
