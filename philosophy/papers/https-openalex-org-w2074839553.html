Lean viewed as a philosophy

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: political science

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Purpose The purpose of this paper is to act as a meticulous conceptual paper probing the contemporary view towards lean and illustrate that, despite its discernible benefits, the implementation record suffers as the prevailing opinion fails to encapsulate that an aspiring lean enterprise shall only succeed if it views lean as a philosophy rather than another strategy.
