Philosophy, Methodology and Action Research

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: action

Secondary subgenres: practical philosophyconsciousnesslaw

Summary: The aim of this paper is to examine the role of methodology in action research. It begins by showing how, as a form of inquiry concerned with the development of practice, action research is nothing other than a modern 20th century manifestation of the pre-modern tradition of practical philosophy. It then draws in Gadamer's powerful vindication of the contemporary relevance of practical philosophy
