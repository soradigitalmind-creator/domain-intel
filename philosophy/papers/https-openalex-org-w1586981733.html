Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: enlightenment

Secondary subgenres: linguisticscritical philosophygerman philosophykantian philosophy and modern interpretations

Summary: Paget introduces the general reader to Afro-Caribbean philosophy in this ground-breaking work. Since Afro-Caribbean thought is inherently hybrid in nature, he traces the roots of this discourse in traditional African thought and in the Christian and Enlightenment traditions of Western Europe.
