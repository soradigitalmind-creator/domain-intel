Introduction to a Philosophy of Music

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: art

Secondary subgenres: art history

Summary: Abstract Philosophy of music has flourished in the last thirty years, with great advances made in the understanding of the nature of music and its aesthetics. Peter Kivy has been at the center of this flourishing, and now offers his personal introduction to philosophy of music, a clear and lively explanation of how he sees the most important and interesting philosophical issues relating to music.
