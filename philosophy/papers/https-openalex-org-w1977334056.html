Qualitative research in counseling psychology: A primer on research paradigms and philosophy of science.

Year: 2005

Primary subgenre: art

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This article presents an overview of philosophy of science and research paradigms. The philosophy of science parameters of ontology, epistemology, axiology, rhetorical structure, and methodology are discussed across the research paradigms of positivism, postpositivism, constructivism-interpretivism, and the critical-ideological perspective. Counseling researchers are urged to locate their inquiry
