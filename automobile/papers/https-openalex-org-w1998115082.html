Partial Ownership Arrangements and Collusion in the Automobile Industry

Year: 1997

Primary subgenre: collusion

Secondary subgenres: commerceindustrial organization

Summary: This paper develops a conjectural variation model that allows for partial ownership arrangements and foreign trade. This model is applied to the Japanese and US automobile industries. Although collusion is found to occur in the Japanese automobile industry, it is found to be more competitive than the US automobile industry.
