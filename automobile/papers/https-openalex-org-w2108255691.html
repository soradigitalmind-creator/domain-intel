A New Approach of Automobile Localization System Using GPS and GSM/GPRS Transmission

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: automatic vehicle location

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This paper presents a low cost automotive localization system using GPS and GSM-SMS services. The system permits localization of the automobile and transmitting the position to the owner on his mobile phone as a short message (SMS) at his request.
