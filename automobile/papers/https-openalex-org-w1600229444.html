The effects of the country of brand and the country of manufacturing of automobiles

Year: 2010

Main topic: automobiles in united

Related topics:

Summary: Purpose The purpose of this paper is to offer a new perspective of country of origin effects on consumers' brand personality perceptions of domestic and imported automobiles. It aims to assess the perceived similarities and differences between automobiles from two countries with respect to the country of origin of the brand (COB) and the country of manufacturing (COM) of that same brand.
