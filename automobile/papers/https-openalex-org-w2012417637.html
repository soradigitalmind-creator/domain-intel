Summary: Within the mobilities literature, there is a growing body of research on the decline of automobility and the emergence of new mobility regimes. In this context, I will outline an understanding of ‘mobility as dispositif’ which facilitates tracing interweavings of discursive knowledge, material structures, social practices and subjectifications around mobilities.
