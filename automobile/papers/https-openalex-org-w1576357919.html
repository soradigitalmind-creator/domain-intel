Hybrid GPS-GSM Localization of Automobile Tracking System

Year: 2011

Primary subgenre: assisted gps

Secondary subgenres: computer science

Summary: An integrated GPS-GSM system is proposed to track vehicles using Google Earth application. The remote module has a GPS mounted on the moving vehicle to identify its current position, and to be transferred by GSM with other parameters acquired by the automobile's data port as an SMS to a recipient station.
