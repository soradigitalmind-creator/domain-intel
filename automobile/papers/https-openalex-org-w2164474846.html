Year: 2005

Primary subgenre: advertising

Secondary subgenres: empirical evidenceempirical researchmarketing

Summary: In a large empirical study, the authors find that older consumers, who constitute an important market segment, repurchase a brand more frequently when they buy a new car. Older consumers consider fewer brands, fewer dealers, and fewer models, and they choose long-established brands more often.
