Energy-harvesting potential of automobile suspension

Year: 2016

Primary subgenre: automotive engineering

Secondary subgenres: electrical engineeringpower

Summary: This study is aimed quantify dissipated power in a damper of automobile suspension to predict energy harvesting potential of a passenger car more accurately. Field measurements of power dissipation in a regenerative damper are still rare. The novelty is in using the broad database of real road profiles, a 9 degrees-of-freedom full-car model with real parameters, and a tyre-enveloping contact model
