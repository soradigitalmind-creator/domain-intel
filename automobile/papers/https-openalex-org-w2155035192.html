Energy efficient use of robotics in the automobile industry

Year: 2011

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This paper summarizes various methods of the energy efficient use of medium and high payload industrial robots. Approaches and according savings potential are evaluated for methods like intelligent brake management - release time and power reduction, the temporal storage of the robot's kinetic energy with capacitive energy buffer of controller's DC-Bus, the energy exchange among robot controllers
