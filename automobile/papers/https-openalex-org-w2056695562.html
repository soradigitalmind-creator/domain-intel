Suppliers' associations in the Japanese automobile industry: collective action for technololgy diffusion

Year: 1996

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This paper analyses the structure and functions of suppliers' associations {kyoryokukai) in the automobile industry in Japan. The bilateral assembler-supplier relationship has received much attention recently as a source of Japanese industrial competitiveness. However, this paper argues that the hitherto neglected area of intersupplier co-ordination in technology diffusion is at least as important
