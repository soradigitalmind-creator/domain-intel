Automobile Anti-theft System Based on GSM and GPS Module

Year: 2012

Primary subgenre: automotive engineering

Secondary subgenres: vehicle tracking system

Summary: In view of automobile anti-theft systems mostly used static real-time detection and alarm at present, in the paper we design an automobile anti-theft system based on GSM and GPS module. The system is developed based on the high speed mixed type single-chip C8051F120 and detect automobile stolen to the automobile owner by vibration sensor.
