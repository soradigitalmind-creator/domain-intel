Year: 1987

Primary subgenre: automotive engineering

Summary: Automobile accidents occurred with 55% of seizures in epileptic drivers with both well-described seizures at the wheel and a well-classified epilepsy diagnosis. A further 38% of seizures would have led to accidents in less fortunate settings. Complex partial seizures occurred in 81% of patients and were responsible for 88% of accidents.
