THE ACCIDENT-PRONE AUTOMOBILE DRIVER

Year: 1949

Main topic: medical emergency

Related topics:

Summary: In this study the existence of accidentprone drivers has been demonstrated in the records of accidents from a bus company extending over a period of 6 years. The frequency of appearance of the same individual in the high accident group in multiple years has been noted.
