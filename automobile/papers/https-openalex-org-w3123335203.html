Service Competition and Product Quality in the U.S. Automobile Industry

Year: 2015

Main topic: asphalt path: automobile

Related topics:

Summary: We study the impact of service attributes (warranty length, after-sales service quality) on consumer demand in the U.S. automobile industry, examining the presence of complementarities/substitution between service attributes and product quality. Our results estimate a median willingness to pay for one year of a warranty of approximately $850, which is equivalent to 3.1% of the median vehicle price
