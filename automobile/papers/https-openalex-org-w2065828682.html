The Impact of WTO Membership on the Automobile Industry in China

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: automotive industry

Secondary subgenres: global trade and economics

Summary: Many areas of China's economy stand to see change from WTO accession, from agriculture to insurance and banking to telecommunications. Among the sectors affected, the automobile industry, as an old-line heavy industry, seems one of the most open to a global challenge. Since its reorganization in the post-Mao Zedong reform era, China's auto industry has been nurtured in a protectionist environment.
