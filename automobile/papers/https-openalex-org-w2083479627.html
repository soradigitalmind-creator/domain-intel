Falling asleep while driving and automobile accidents among patients with obstructive sleep apnea–hypopnea syndrome

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: anesthesia

Secondary subgenres: apneaemergency medicinemedicine

Summary: Among 448 patients with obstructive sleep apnea-hypopnea syndrome (OSAHS), 40 patients (8.9%) had been involved in one or more automobile accidents during the preceding 5 years. The main cause of these accidents was falling asleep while driving.
