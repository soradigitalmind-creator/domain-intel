Year: 1992

Primary subgenre: actuarial science

Secondary subgenres: automobile insurancebusinesseconomics

Summary: We begin by providing an overview of the auto insurance system and the structure of the auto insurance market. We then turn to an analysis of the factors underlying the auto insurance price increases experienced in recent years.
