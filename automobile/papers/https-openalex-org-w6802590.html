Summary: Household location decisions are closely related to other choices of housing, automobile ownership, and mode to work. This paper describes a model that considers these long-run decisions, termed the mobility bundle, as jointly determined, thus eliminating the need for an arbitary set of assumptions about a sequence of choices.
