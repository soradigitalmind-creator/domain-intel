The Incentive Effects of No‐Fault Automobile Insurance

Year: 2001

Main topic: automobiles in united

Related topics:

Summary: This paper presents a theoretical and empirical analysis of the effects of no‐fault automobile insurance on fatal accident rates. As a mechanism for compensating the victims of automobile accidents, no‐fault insurance has several important advantages over the tort system.
