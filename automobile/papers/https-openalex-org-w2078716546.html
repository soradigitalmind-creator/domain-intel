Lead Poisoning in Automobile Radiator Mechanics

Year: 1987

Primary subgenre: mechanical engineering

Secondary subgenres: mechanics

Summary: Exposure to lead occurs during automobile radiator repair when soldered joints are heated, but this relatively common hazard has received little public recognition. We therefore studied lead exposure among automobile radiator mechanics in the Boston area. Twenty-seven shops were surveyed, and most were found to be small and poorly ventilated.
