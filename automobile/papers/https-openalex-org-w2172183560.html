Vision-based vehicle detection and tracking method for forward collision warning in automobiles

Year: 2003

Primary subgenre: vehicle tracking system

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We describe a vision-based vehicle detection and tracking method for forward collision warning in automobiles. The approach is based on a set of edge-based constraint filters that assist in the segmentation of vehicles from background clutter.
