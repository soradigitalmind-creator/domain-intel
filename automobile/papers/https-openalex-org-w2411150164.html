The microsociology of automobility: the production of the automobile self

Year: 2016

Main topic: automobiles in united

Related topics:

Summary: Drivers and automobiles are frequently represented in the automobility studies literature as hybrid human–machine cyborg assemblages. A concurrent theme within the automobility literature has been the disciplinary processes by which the automobile self is constructed; here, however, it is assumed that the self to be investigated is the self of the driver, not the self of the car-driver entity.
