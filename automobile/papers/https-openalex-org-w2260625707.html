Fatigue, Sleep Restriction, and Performance in Automobile Drivers: A Controlled Study in a Natural Environment

Year: 2003

Primary subgenre: medical emergency

Secondary subgenres: poison controlcontrol

Summary: Sleep restriction combined with fatigue significantly affects reaction time. The lack of correspondence between reaction time and prospective self-evaluation of performance suggests that self-monitoring in real conditions is poorly reliable.
