Adjustment of Consumers' Durables Stocks: Evidence from Automobile Purchases

Year: 1994

Primary subgenre: aggregate

Secondary subgenres: econometricseconomics

Summary: This paper tests an optimal (S, s) rule in household durable purchases and examines directly the resulting aggregate expenditure dynamics. The observed decision rule responds to income uncertainty and growth as predicted by an (S, s) model resulting from transactions costs.
