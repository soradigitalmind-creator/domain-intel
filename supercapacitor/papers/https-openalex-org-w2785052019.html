Abstract
Supercapacitors represent a major technology to store energy for many applications including electronics, automobiles, military, and space. Despite their high power density, the energy density in supercapacitors is presently inferior to that of the state-of-the-art Li-ion batteries owing to the limited electrochemical performance exhibited by the conventional electrode materials.
