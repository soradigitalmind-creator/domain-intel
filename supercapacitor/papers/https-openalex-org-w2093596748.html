Abstract
For building high‐energy density asymmetric supercapacitors, developing anode materials with large specific capacitance remains a great challenge. Although Fe 2 O 3 has been considered as a promising anode material for asymmetric supercapacitors, the specific capacitance of the Fe 2 O 3 ‐based anodes is still low and cannot match that of cathodes in the full cells.
