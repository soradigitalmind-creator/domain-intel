Surface energy and work function of elemental metals

Year: 1992

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: We have performed an ab initio study of the surface energy and the work function for six close-packed surfaces of 40 elemental metals by means of a Green's-function technique, based on the linear-muffin-tin-orbitals method within the tight-binding and atomic-sphere approximations.
