Summary: International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) safeguards inspections in nuclear facilities to verify that the fissionable material is used for peaceful purposes only. Many of the IAEA programmes contribute directly or indirectly to the goals of sustainable development and protection of the environment as set out in Agenda 21, the outcome of the 1992 UN Conference on Environment and Development.
