Binding energies in atomic negative ions

Year: 1975

Primary subgenre:

Summary: A survey of the electron affinity determinations for the elements up to Z=85 is presented, and based upon these data, a set of recommended electron affinities is established. Recent calculations of atomic electron affinities and the major semiempirical methods are discussed and compared with experiment.
