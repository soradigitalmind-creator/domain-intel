Estimation of the surface free energy of polymers

Year: 1969

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: Abstract A method for measuring the surface energy of solids and for resolving the surface energy into contributions from dispersion and dipole‐hydrogen bonding forces has been developed. It is based on the measurement of contact angles with water and methylene iodide. Good agreement has been obtained with the more laborious γ c method.
