gmx_MMPBSA: A New Tool to Perform End-State Free Energy Calculations with GROMACS

Year: 2021

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: Molecular mechanics/Poisson-Boltzmann (Generalized-Born) surface area is one of the most popular methods to estimate binding free energies. This method has been proven to balance accuracy and computational efficiency, especially when dealing with large systems. As a result of its popularity, several programs have been developed for performing MM/PB(GB)SA calculations within the GROMACS community.
