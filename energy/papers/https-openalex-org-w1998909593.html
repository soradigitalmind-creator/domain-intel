Radiative energy loss of high energy quarks and gluons in a finite-volume quark-gluon plasma

Year: 1997

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: The medium induced energy loss spectrum of a quark or gluon of high energy E traversing a hot QCD medium of finite volume is studied. We model the interaction by a simple picture of static scattering centres. The total induced energy loss is found to grow as L2, where L is the extent of the medium.
