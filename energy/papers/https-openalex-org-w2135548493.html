A micro electromagnetic generator for vibration energy harvesting

Year: 2007

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Summary: Vibration energy harvesting is receiving a considerable amount of interest as a means for powering wireless sensor nodes. This paper presents a small (component volume 0.1 cm3, practical volume 0.15 cm3) electromagnetic generator utilizing discrete components and optimized for a low ambient vibration level based upon real application data.
