Energy Storage and Its Use With Intermittent Renewable Energy

Year: 2004

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: A simple probabilistic method has been developed to predict the ability of energy storage to increase the penetration of intermittent embedded renewable generation (ERG) on weak electricity grids and to enhance the value of the electricity generated by time-shifting delivery to the network. This paper focuses on the connection of wind generators at locations where the level of ERG would be limited
