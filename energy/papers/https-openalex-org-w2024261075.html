Quantifying Defects in Graphene via Raman Spectroscopy at Different Excitation Energies

Year: 2011

Main topic: wind energy

Related topics:

Summary: We present a Raman study of Ar(+)-bombarded graphene samples with increasing ion doses. This allows us to have a controlled, increasing, amount of defects. We find that the ratio between the D and G peak intensities, for a given defect density, strongly depends on the laser excitation energy.
