Approximate fourth‐order perturbation theory of the electron correlation energy

Year: 1978

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: Abstract An approximate fourth‐order expression for the electron correlation energy in the Møller–Plesset perturbation scheme is proposed. It takes into account all the contributions to the fourthorder energy neglecting only those of the triple‐substituted determinants.
