Efficiency of hot-carrier solar energy converters

Year: 1982

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A single-threshold quantum-utilizing device in which the excited carriers thermally equilibrate among themselves, but not with the environment, can convert solar energy with an efficiency approaching that of an infinite-threshold device. Such a hot-carrier flat-plate device operated under typical terrestrial conditions (AM 1.5 illumination, 300 K) can convert solar energy with an efficiency of 66%
