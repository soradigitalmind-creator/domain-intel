The absolute energy positions of conduction and valence bands of selected semiconducting minerals

Year: 2000

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: The absolute energy positions of conduction and valence band edges were compiled for about 50 each semiconducting metal oxide and metal sulfide minerals. The relationships between energy levels at mineral semiconductor-electrolyte interfaces and the activities of these minerals as a catalyst or photocatalyst in aqueous redox reactions are reviewed.
