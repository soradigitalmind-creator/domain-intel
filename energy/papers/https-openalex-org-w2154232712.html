Electrical Energy Storage and Intercalation Chemistry

Year: 1976

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: The electrochemical reaction of layered titanium disulfide with lithium giving the intercalation compound lithium titanium disulfide is the basis of a new battery system. This reaction occurs very rapidly and in a highly reversible manner at ambient temperatures as a result of structural retention. Titanium disulfide is one of a new generation of solid cathode materials.
