Year: 1999

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: This article describes the derivation of MMFF94s, which is the "s" (static) variant of MMFF94. MMFF94s incorporates altered out of plane bending parameters that yield planar (or nearly planar) energy-minimized geometries at unstrained delocalized trigonal nitrogen centers.
