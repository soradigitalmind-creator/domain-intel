Quadratic configuration interaction. A general technique for determining electron correlation energies

Year: 1987

Main topic: wind energy

Related topics:

Summary: A general procedure is introduced for calculation of the electron correlation energy, starting from a single Hartree–Fock determinant. The normal equations of (linear) configuration interaction theory are modified by introducing new terms which are quadratic in the configuration coefficients and which ensure size consistency in the resulting total energy.
