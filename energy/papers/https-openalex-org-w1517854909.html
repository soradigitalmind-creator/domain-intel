A manual for the economic evaluation of energy efficiency and renewable energy technologies

Year: 1995

Main topic: atomic energy agency

Related topics:

Summary: This manual is a guide for analyzing the economics of energy efficiency and renewable energy (EE) technologies and projects. It is intended (1) to help analysts determine the appropriate approach or type of analysis and the appropriate level of detail and (2) to assist EE analysts in completing consistent analyses using standard assumptions and bases, when appropriate.
