Rainfall energy and its relationship to soil loss

Year: 1958

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A relatively simple procedure is presented for computation of kinetic energy of a rainstorm from information on a recording‐raingage chart. An equation is developed describing rainfall energy as a function of rainfall intensity.
