Prostate-specific membrane antigen expression in normal and malignant human tissues.

Year: 1997

Main topic: membrane protein

Abstract
Prostate-specific membrane antigen is a type II membrane protein with folate hydrolase activity produced by prostatic epithelium. The expression of this molecule has also been documented in extraprostatic tissues, including small bowel and brain. In the present study, an extensive immunohistochemical analysis was performed on a panel of well-characterized normal and malignant human tissues to further define the pattern of prostate-specific membrane antigen (PSMA) expression.
