A platelet alpha-granule membrane protein (GMP-140) is expressed on the plasma membrane after activation.

Year: 1985  DOI ↗

Main topic: membrane protein

Abstract
We have previously characterized a monoclonal antibody, S12, that binds only to activated platelets (McEver, R.P., and M.N. Martin, 1984, J. Biol. Chem., 259:9799-9804). It identifies a platelet membrane protein of Mr 140,000, which we have designated as GMP-140. Using immunocytochemical techniques we have now localized this protein in unstimulated and thrombin-stimulated platelets.
