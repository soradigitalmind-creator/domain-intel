MnO<sub>2</sub>-Based Nanostructures as Catalysts for Electrochemical Oxygen Reduction in Alkaline Media

Year: 2009

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: This paper reports a systematical study on the electrochemical properties of MnO2-based nanostructures as low-cost catalysts for oxygen reduction reaction (ORR) in alkaline media. The results show that the catalytic activities of MnO2 depend strongly on the crystallographic structures, following an order of α- > β- > γ-MnO2.
