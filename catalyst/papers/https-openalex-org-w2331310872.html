Magnetic Nanoscaled Fe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>4</sub>/CeO<sub>2</sub> Composite as an Efficient Fenton-Like Heterogeneous Catalyst for Degradation of 4-Chlorophenol

Year: 2012

Primary subgenre: hydrogen peroxide

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Magnetic nanoscaled Fe(3)O(4)/CeO(2) composite was prepared by the impregnation method and characterized as a heterogeneous Fenton-like catalyst for 4-chlorophenol (4-CP) degradation. The catalytic activity was evaluated in view of the effects of various processes, pH value, catalyst addition, hydrogen peroxide (H(2)O(2)) concentration, and temperature, and the pseudo-first-order kinetic constant
