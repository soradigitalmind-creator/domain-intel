Ni–C–N Nanosheets as Catalyst for Hydrogen Evolution Reaction

Year: 2016

Primary subgenre: nanomaterial-based catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We report a facile nitrogenation/exfoliation process to prepare hybrid Ni-C-N nanosheets. These nanosheets are <2 nm thin, chemically stable, and metallically conductive. They serve as a robust catalyst for the hydrogen evolution reaction in 0.5 M H<sub>2</sub>SO<sub>4</sub>, or 1.0 M KOH or 1.0 M PBS (pH = 7).
