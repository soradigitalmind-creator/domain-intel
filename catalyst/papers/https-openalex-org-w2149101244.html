A metal–organic framework material that functions as an enantioselective catalyst for olefin epoxidation

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: olefin fiber

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A new microporous metal-organic framework compound featuring chiral (salen)Mn struts is highly effective as an asymmetric catalyst for olefin epoxidation, yielding enantiomeric excesses that rival those of the free molecular analogue. Framework confinement of the manganese salen entity enhances catalyst stability, imparts substrate size selectivity, and permits catalyst separation and reuse.
