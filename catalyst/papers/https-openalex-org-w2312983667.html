Nanostructured Tin Catalysts for Selective Electrochemical Reduction of Carbon Dioxide to Formate

Year: 2014

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: High surface area tin oxide nanocrystals prepared by a facile hydrothermal method are evaluated as electrocatalysts toward CO2 reduction to formate. At these novel nanostructured tin catalysts, CO2 reduction occurs selectively to formate at overpotentials as low as ∼340 mV.
