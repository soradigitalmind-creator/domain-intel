Computational Design of an Enzyme Catalyst for a Stereoselective Bimolecular Diels-Alder Reaction

Year: 2010  DOI ↗

Main topic: combinatorial chemistry

Related topics:

Abstract
The Diels-Alder reaction is a cornerstone in organic synthesis, forming two carbon-carbon bonds and up to four new stereogenic centers in one step. No naturally occurring enzymes have been shown to catalyze bimolecular Diels-Alder reactions. We describe the de novo computational design and experimental characterization of enzymes catalyzing a bimolecular Diels-Alder reaction with high stereoselectivity and substrate specificity.
