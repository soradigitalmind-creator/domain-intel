Zeolites: Catalysts for Organic Syntheses

Year: 1988

Primary subgenre: nanomaterial-based catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Abstract Zeolites have proved to be valuable technical catalysts in petrochemistry and in oil processing. The characteristic properties of zeolites, such as acidity, shape‐selectivity and thermal stability also enable them to be used for highly selective synthesis in the fields of chemical intermediates and fine chemicals. This interesting area of application has grown continuously in recent years
