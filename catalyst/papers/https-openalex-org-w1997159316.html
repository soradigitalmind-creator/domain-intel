Highly Active Palladium Catalysts for Suzuki Coupling Reactions

Year: 1999

Primary subgenre: palladium catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Mixtures of palladium acetate and o-(di-tert-butylphosphino)biphenyl (4) catalyze the room-temperature Suzuki coupling of aryl bromides and aryl chlorides with 0.5−1.0 mol % Pd. Use of o-(dicyclohexylphosphino)biphenyl (2) allows Suzuki couplings to be carried out at low catalyst loadings (0.000001−0.02 mol % Pd). The process tolerates a broad range of functional groups and substrate combinations
