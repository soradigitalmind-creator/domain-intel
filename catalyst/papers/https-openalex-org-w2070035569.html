Cyclometalated Iridium(III) Aquo Complexes:  Efficient and Tunable Catalysts for the Homogeneous Oxidation of Water

Year: 2007

Primary subgenre: nanomaterial-based catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A series of bis-phenylpyridine, bis-aquo iridium(III) complexes is herein shown to robustly and efficiently catalyze the oxidation of water to dioxygen in the presence of a sacrificial oxidant. Through substitution on the cyclometalating ligands of these complexes, it is shown that a broad range of oxidation potentials can be achieved within this class of catalyst.
