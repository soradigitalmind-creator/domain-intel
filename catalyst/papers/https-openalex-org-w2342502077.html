Formic acid as a hydrogen storage material – development of homogeneous catalysts for selective hydrogen release

Year: 2016

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Formic acid (FA, HCO2H) receives considerable attention as a hydrogen storage material. In this respect, hydrogenation of CO2 to FA and dehydrogenation of FA are crucial reaction steps. In the past decade, for both reactions, several molecularly defined and nanostructured catalysts have been developed and intensively studied.
