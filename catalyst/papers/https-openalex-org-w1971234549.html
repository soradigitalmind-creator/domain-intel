A General and Long-Lived Catalyst for the Palladium-Catalyzed Coupling of Aryl Halides with Thiols

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: palladium catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A general catalytic system for the coupling of aryl halides and sulfonates with thiols based on the use of the CyPF-t-Bu ligand (1) is reported. The reactions catalyzed by complexes of 1 occur in excellent yields with broad scope and exhibit extraordinary turnover numbers and high tolerance of functional groups.
