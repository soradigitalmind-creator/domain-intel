State of the Art of Catalysts for Biodiesel Production

Year: 2020  DOI ↗

Main topic: heterogeneous catalysis

Abstract
Biodiesel is one of the potential alternative energy sources that can be derived from renewable and low-grade origin through different processes. One of the processes is alcoholysis or transesterification in the presence of a suitable catalyst. The catalyst can be either homogeneous or heterogeneous.
