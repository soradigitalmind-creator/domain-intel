The Heck Reaction in Ionic Liquids:  A Multiphasic Catalyst System

Year: 1999

Primary subgenre: palladium catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Heck coupling of aryl halides or benzoic anhydride with alkenes can be performed with excellent yields in room-temperature ionic liquids, which provide a medium that dissolves the palladium catalyst and allows the product and byproducts to be easily separated. Consequently, the catalyst and ionic liquid can be recycled and reused.
