An Extremely Active Catalyst for the Negishi Cross-Coupling Reaction

Year: 2004

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A new catalyst system for the Pd-catalyzed cross-coupling of organozinc reagents with aryl halides (Negishi coupling) has been developed. This system permits efficient preparation of hindered biaryls (tri- and tetra-ortho-substituted), functions effectively at low levels of catalyst, and tolerates a wide range of functional groups and heterocyclic substrates.
