Structure–Activity Correlations in a Nickel–Borate Oxygen Evolution Catalyst

Year: 2012

Primary subgenre: nanomaterial-based catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: An oxygen evolution catalyst that forms as a thin film from Ni(aq)(2+) solutions containing borate electrolyte (Ni-B(i)) has been studied by in situ X-ray absorption spectroscopy. A dramatic increase in catalytic rate, induced by anodic activation of the electrodeposited films, is accompanied by structure and oxidation state changes.
