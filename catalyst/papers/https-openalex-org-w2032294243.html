An Efficient and Chemoselective Iron Catalyst for the Hydrogenation of Ketones

Year: 2007

Primary subgenre: bifunctional catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Iron complex 1 containing electronically coupled acidic and hydridic hydrogens catalyzes the hydrogenation of ketones under mild conditions. This hydrogenation catalyst shows high chemoselectivity for aldehydes, ketones, and imines, and isolated CC, C⋮C, C−X, −NO2, epoxides, and ester functions are unaffected by the hydrogenation conditions.
