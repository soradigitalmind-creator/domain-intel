Oxidation of glycerol using supported Pt, Pd and Au catalysts

Year: 2003

Primary subgenre: nanomaterial-based catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: The oxidation of aqueous solutions of glycerol is described and discussed for Pd, Pt and Au nanoparticles supported on graphite and activated carbon. The oxidation in a batch reactor at 60 °C and 1 bar pressure using air as oxidant was initially investigated. Under these conditions, supported Pd and Pt catalysts give some selectivity to glyceric acid, but the main reaction products are considered
