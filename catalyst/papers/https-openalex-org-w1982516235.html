Conversion of Sugars to Lactic Acid Derivatives Using Heterogeneous Zeotype Catalysts

Year: 2010

Primary subgenre: nanomaterial-based catalyst

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Presently, very few compounds of commercial interest are directly accessible from carbohydrates by using nonfermentive approaches. We describe here a catalytic process for the direct formation of methyl lactate from common sugars.
