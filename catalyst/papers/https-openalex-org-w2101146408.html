Evaluation of Pore Structure Parameters of MCM-41 Catalyst Supports and Catalysts by Means of Nitrogen and Argon Adsorption

Year: 1997

Primary subgenre: catalyst support

Secondary subgenres: bifunctional catalystcarbon nanotube supported catalystnanomaterial-based catalystpalladium catalyst

Summary: A new method has been used to obtain pore size characteristics of MCM-41 catalyst supports and vanadium-substituted MCM-41 catalysts. The approach is based on the nonlocal density functional theory (NLDFT) model for nitrogen and argon adsorption in MCM-41, proposed recently.
