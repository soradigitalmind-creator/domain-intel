Main topic: field-effect transistor

Abstract
Abstract Light emitting field effect transistors based on molecular and polymeric organic semiconductors are multifunctional devices that integrate light emission with the current modulating function of a transistor. The planar geometry of organic light emitting field effect transistors (OLEFETs) offers direct access to the light emission region, providing a unique experimental configuration to investigate fundamental optical and electronic properties in organic semiconductors.
