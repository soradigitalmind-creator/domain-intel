Abstract
Single ZnO nanowire metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors (MOSFETs) were fabricated using nanowires grown by site selective molecular-beam epitaxy. When measured in the dark at 25°C, he depletion-mode transistors exhibit good saturation behavior, a threshold voltage of ∼−3V, and a maximum transconductance of order 0.3mS∕mm.
