Characterization of a Novel Nine-Transistor SRAM Cell

Year: 2008  DOI ↗

Main topic: electronic engineering

Abstract
Data stability of SRAM cells has become an important issue with the scaling of CMOS technology. Memory banks are also important sources of leakage since the majority of transistors are utilized for on-chip caches in today's high performance microprocessors. A new nine-transistor (9T) SRAM cell is proposed in this paper for simultaneously reducing leakage power and enhancing data stability.
