Thermodynamic properties of strong electrolytes in aqueous solutions

Year: 1973  DOI ↗

Main topic: aqueous solution

Abstract
Abstract A generalized analytic correlation is presented for activity coefficient. osmotic coefficient, enthalpy, and heat capacity of single and multicomponent strong aqueous solutions. A good correlation for each salt to an ionic strength of six is obtained by assigning a single parameter “ B ” value to each salt. These B values are well approximated by assigning two parameters for each ion. Values presented for the common ions at 25°C allow the estimation of activity and osmotic coefficients
