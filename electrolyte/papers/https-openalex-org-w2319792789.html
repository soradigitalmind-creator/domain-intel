Abstract
Lithium garnet with the formula Li7La3Zr2O12 (LLZO) has many properties of an ideal electrolyte in all-solid state lithium batteries. However, internal resistance in batteries utilizing these electrolytes remains high. For widespread adoption, the LLZO’s internal resistance must be lowered by increasing its bulk conductivity, reducing grain boundary resistance, and/or pairing it with an appropriate cathode to minimize interfacial resistance.
