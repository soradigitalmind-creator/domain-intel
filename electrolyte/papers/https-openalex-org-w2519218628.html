Cation-Deficient Spinel ZnMn<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub> Cathode in Zn(CF<sub>3</sub>SO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub> Electrolyte for Rechargeable Aqueous Zn-Ion Battery

Year: 2016  DOI ↗

Main topic: aqueous solution

Abstract
Rechargeable aqueous Zn-ion batteries are attractive cheap, safe and green energy storage technologies but are bottlenecked by limitation in high-capacity cathode and compatible electrolyte to achieve satisfactory cyclability. Here we report the application of nonstoichiometric ZnMn<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>/carbon composite as a new Zn-insertion cathode material in aqueous Zn(CF<sub>3</sub>SO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub> electrolyte.
