A Laser Ablation Method for the Synthesis of Crystalline Semiconductor Nanowires

Year: 1998

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A method combining laser ablation cluster formation and vapor-liquid-solid (VLS) growth was developed for the synthesis of semiconductor nanowires. In this process, laser ablation was used to prepare nanometer-diameter catalyst clusters that define the size of wires produced by VLS growth.
