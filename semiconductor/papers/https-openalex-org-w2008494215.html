First principles materials design for semiconductor spintronics

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: magnetic semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: magnetic fieldzno doping and propertymagnetizationferromagnetism

Summary: Materials design of new functional diluted magnetic semiconductors (DMSs) is presented based on first principles calculations. The stability of the ferromagnetic state in ZnO-, ZnS-, ZnSe-, ZnTe-, GaAs- and GaN-based DMSs is investigated systematically and it is suggested that V- or Cr-doped ZnO, ZnS, ZnSe and ZnTe are candidates for high-TC ferromagnetic DMSs.
