Condensation of Semiconductor Microcavity Exciton Polaritons

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: optical microcavity

Secondary subgenres: strong light-matter interactionspolar

Summary: A phase transition from a classical thermal mixed state to a quantum-mechanical pure state of exciton polaritons is observed in a GaAs multiple quantum-well microcavity from the decrease of the second-order coherence function. Supporting evidence is obtained from the observation of a nonlinear threshold behavior in the pump-intensity dependence of the emission, a polariton-like dispersion relation
