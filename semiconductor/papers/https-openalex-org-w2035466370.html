Stimulated emission in semiconductor quantum wire heterostructures

Year: 1989

Primary subgenre: semiconductor quantum structures and devices

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We report the first observation of stimulated emission in quasi-one-dimensional semiconductor quantum wires. Amplified spontaneous emission and stimulated emission spectra of the GaAs/AlGaAs quantum wires exhibit fine structure arising from transitions between lateral, one-dimensional electron and hole subbands.
