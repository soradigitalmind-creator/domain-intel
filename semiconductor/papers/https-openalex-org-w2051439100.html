Random Laser Action in Semiconductor Powder

Year: 1999

Primary subgenre: light scattering

Secondary subgenres: semiconductor laser theorycompound semiconductorintrinsic semiconductor

Summary: We report the first observation of random laser action with coherent feedback in semiconductor powder. Since the scattering mean free path is less than the emission wavelength, recurrent light scattering arises and provides coherent feedback for lasing.
