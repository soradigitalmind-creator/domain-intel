Room-Temperature Polariton Lasing in Semiconductor Microcavities

Year: 2007

Primary subgenre: strong light-matter interactions

Summary: We observe a room-temperature low-threshold transition to a coherent polariton state in bulk GaN microcavities in the strong-coupling regime.
