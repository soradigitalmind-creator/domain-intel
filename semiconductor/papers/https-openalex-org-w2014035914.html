Quantum Information Processing with Semiconductor Macroatoms

Year: 2000

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: An all optical implementation of quantum information processing with semiconductor macroatoms is proposed. Our quantum hardware consists of an array of quantum dots and the computational degrees of freedom are energy-selected interband optical transitions. The quantum-computing strategy exploits exciton-exciton interactions driven by ultrafast multicolor laser pulses. Contrary to existing proposal
