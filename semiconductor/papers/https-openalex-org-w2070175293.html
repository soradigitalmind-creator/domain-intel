The Hall effect in polycrystalline and powdered semiconductors

Year: 1980

Primary subgenre: electrical resistivity and conductivity

Secondary subgenres: hall effect

Summary: Gives a critical review of idealised two-phase geometrical models. These treatments derive expressions for the resistivity and Hall coefficient of a composite material in terms of the properties of its constituents.
