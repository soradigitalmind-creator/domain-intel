Year: 2000

Primary subgenre: zno doping and property

Secondary subgenres: wide-bandgap semiconductor

Summary: Ordered semiconductor ZnO nanowire arrays embedded in anodic alumina membranes (AAM) were fabricated by generating alumina templates with nanochannels, electrodepositing Zn in them, and then oxidizing the Zn nanowire arrays.
