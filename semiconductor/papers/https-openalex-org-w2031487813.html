Nonlinear optical susceptibilities of semiconductors: Results with a length-gauge analysis

Year: 1995

Primary subgenre:

Summary: We present a simple prescription for the derivation of electronic contributions to the nonlinear optical response of crystals in the independent particle approximation. Semiconductor Bloch equations are found that include previously neglected effects of intraband motion.
