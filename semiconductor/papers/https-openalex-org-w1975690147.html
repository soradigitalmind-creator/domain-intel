Conditions for efficient spin injection from a ferromagnetic metal into a semiconductor

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: magnetic field

Secondary subgenres: magnetic property of thin filmsspin diffusion

Summary: We adapt the spin accumulation model of the perpendicular transport in metallic magnetic multilayers to the issue of spin injection from a ferromagnetic metal (F) into a semiconductor (N). We show that the problem of the conductivity mismatch between F and N can be solved by introducing a spin dependent interface resistance (tunnel junction preferably) at the $F/N$ interfaces.
