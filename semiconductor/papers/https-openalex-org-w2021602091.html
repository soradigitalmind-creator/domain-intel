Summary: The band-to-band optical absorption is calculated for a direct-band-gap semiconductor that has one band degenerate $n$ or $p$ type. The degenerate band is treated as a high-density Fermi gas. It is shown that exciton states, arising from the electron-hole Coulomb attraction, still affect the optical absorption.
