Enhancement of Carrier Mobility in Semiconductor Nanostructures by Dielectric Engineering

Year: 2007

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: intrinsic semiconductor

Summary: We propose a technique for achieving large improvements in carrier mobilities in 2- and 1-dimensional semiconductor nanostructures by modifying their dielectric environments. We show that by coating the nanostructures with high-kappa dielectrics, scattering from Coulombic impurities can be strongly damped.
