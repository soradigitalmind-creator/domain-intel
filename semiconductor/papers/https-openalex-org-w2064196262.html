Elastic Properties of ZnS Structure Semiconductors

Year: 1970

Primary subgenre: molecule

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A simple phenomenological theory of the elastic constants of sphalerite structure crystals is presented and shown to apply within reasonable errors to the known experimental constants. The theory utilizes a form for bond-stretching ($\ensuremath{\alpha}$) and bending ($\ensuremath{\beta}$) forces first used by Keating, to which are added effective point-ion Coulombic forces.
