First-Principles Theory of Quasiparticles: Calculation of Band Gaps in Semiconductors and Insulators

Year: 1985

Primary subgenre: band gap

Secondary subgenres: electronic band structure

Summary: We present a first-principles theory for the quasiparticle energies of semiconductors and insulators. The full dielectric matrix is used to evaluate the nonlocal, energy-dependent electron self-energy operator.
