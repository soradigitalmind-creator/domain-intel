Physics of strain effects in semiconductors and metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors

Year: 2007

Primary subgenre: field-effect transistor

Secondary subgenres: transistorband gapsemimetal

Summary: A detailed theoretical picture is given for the physics of strain effects in bulk semiconductors and surface Si, Ge, and III–V channel metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors.
