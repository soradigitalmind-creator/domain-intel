Polaron Percolation in Diluted Magnetic Semiconductors

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: magnetic semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: electrical resistivity and conductivitymagnetic fieldmagnetizationpolaron

Summary: We theoretically study the development of spontaneous magnetization in diluted magnetic semiconductors as arising from a percolation of bound magnetic polarons. Within the framework of a generalized percolation theory we derive analytic expressions for the Curie temperature and the magnetization in the limit of low carrier density, obtaining excellent quantitative agreement with Monte Carlo simula
