Terahertz Near-Field Nanoscopy of Mobile Carriers in Single Semiconductor Nanodevices

Year: 2008

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We introduce ultraresolving terahertz (THz) near-field microscopy based on THz scattering at atomic force microscope tips. Nanoscale resolution is achieved by THz field confinement at the very tip apex to within 30 nm, which is in good agreement with full electro-dynamic calculations. Imaging semiconductor transistors, we provide first evidence of 40 nm (lambda/3000) spatial resolution at 2.54 THz
