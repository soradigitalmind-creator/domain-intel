Simple technique for separating the effects of interface traps and trapped-oxide charge in metal-oxide-semiconductor transistors

Year: 1986

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: field-effect transistortransistorsemiconductor material

Summary: A new technique is presented for separating the threshold-voltage shift of a metal-oxide-semiconductor transistor into shifts due to interface traps and trapped-oxide charge. This technique is applied to threshold-voltage shifts on an n-channel transistor that result from ionizing radiation.
