First-principles theory of dilute magnetic semiconductors

Year: 2010

Primary subgenre: density functional theory

Secondary subgenres: magnetismspintronicszno doping and propertyferromagnetism

Summary: This review summarizes recent first-principles investigations of the electronic structure and magnetism of dilute magnetic semiconductors (DMSs), which are interesting for applications in spintronics. Details of the electronic structure of transition-metal-doped III-V and II-VI semiconductors are described, especially how the electronic structure couples to the magnetic properties of an impurity.
