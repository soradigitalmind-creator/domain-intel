High-speed modulation of semiconductor lasers

Year: 1985

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: An overview is given of the direct modulation performance of high-speed semiconductor lasers. The high-speed response characteristics are described using a cascaded two-port model of the laser. This model separates the electrical parasitics from the intrinsic laser and enables these subsections to be considered separately.
