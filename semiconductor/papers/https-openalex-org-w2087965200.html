Coherent Manipulation of Coupled Electron Spins in Semiconductor Quantum Dots

Year: 2005

Primary subgenre: singlet state

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We demonstrated coherent control of a quantum two-level system based on two-electron spin states in a double quantum dot, allowing state preparation, coherent manipulation, and projective readout. These techniques are based on rapid electrical control of the exchange interaction.
