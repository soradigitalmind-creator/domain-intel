Heteroarenes as high performance organic semiconductors

Year: 2013

Primary subgenre: conjugated system

Secondary subgenres: organic chemistrypolymerorganic electronics and photovoltaicsorganic semiconductor

Summary: The design, synthesis, and characterization of new organic semiconductors (OSCs) are important aspects for the development of next-generation optoelectronic devices. Structurally, organic semiconductors based on π-conjugated molecules can be easily modified via rational synthesis to tune multi-level self-assembled structures and discover novel chemical, optical, and electronic properties.
