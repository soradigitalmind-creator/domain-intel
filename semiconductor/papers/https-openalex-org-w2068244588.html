Shortest wavelength semiconductor laser diode

Year: 1996

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A group III, nitride based, separate confinement heterostructure (SCH) single quantum well (SQW) structure, with an active layer thickness as small as 1.5 nm, was fabricated. It shows the shortest lasing from semiconductor lasers by current injection at room temperature to date. Line width is as little as 0.15 nm.
