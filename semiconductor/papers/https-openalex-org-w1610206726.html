Mg x Zn 1−x O as a II–VI widegap semiconductor alloy

Year: 1998

Primary subgenre: wide-bandgap semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: ga2o3 and related materialultravioletpulsed laser deposition

Summary: We propose a widegap II–VI semiconductor alloy, MgxZn1−xO, for the fabrication of heteroepitaxial ultraviolet light emitting devices based on ZnO. The c-axis oriented MgxZn1−xO films were epitaxially grown by pulsed laser deposition on ZnO epitaxial films and sapphire (0001) substrates using ceramic targets.
