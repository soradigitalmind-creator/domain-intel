Optical Properties of Semiconductors

Year: 1963

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Reflectance data are presented for Si, Ge, GaP, GaAs, InAs, and InSb in the range of photon energies between 1.5 and 25 eV. The real and imaginary parts of the dielectric constant and the function describing the energy loss of fast electrons traversing the materials are deduced from the Kramers-Kronig relations. The results can be described in terms of interband transitions and plasma oscillations
