DIRECT DETERMINATION OF OPTICAL GAIN IN SEMICONDUCTOR CRYSTALS

Year: 1971

Primary subgenre: intrinsic semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: compound semiconductor

Summary: We report a new technique for measuring the stimulated emission spectrum and optical gain of semiconductor materials. Amplified spontaneous emission is used to determine the gain factor by relating the measured variation in light output to variation in the length of the excitation beam.
