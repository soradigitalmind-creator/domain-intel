Observation of discrete electronic states in a zero-dimensional semiconductor nanostructure

Year: 1988

Primary subgenre: semiconductor nanostructures

Secondary subgenres: compound semiconductorintrinsic semiconductor

Summary: Electronic transport through a three-dimensionally confined semiconductor quantum well (``quantum dot'') has been investigated. Fine structure observed in resonant tunneling through the quantum dot corresponds to the discrete density of states of a zero-dimensional system.
