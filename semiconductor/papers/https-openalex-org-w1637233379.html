An All-Optical Quantum Gate in a Semiconductor Quantum Dot

Year: 2003

Primary subgenre: semiconductor quantum structures and devices

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We report coherent optical control of a biexciton (two electron-hole pairs), confined in a single quantum dot, that shows coherent oscillations similar to the excited-state Rabi flopping in an isolated atom.
