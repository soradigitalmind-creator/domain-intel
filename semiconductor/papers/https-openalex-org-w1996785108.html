Band offsets of high K gate oxides on III-V semiconductors

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: wide-bandgap semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: compound semiconductor

Summary: III-V semiconductors have high mobility and will be used in field effect transistors with the appropriate gate dielectric. The dielectrics must have band offsets over 1eV to inhibit leakage.
