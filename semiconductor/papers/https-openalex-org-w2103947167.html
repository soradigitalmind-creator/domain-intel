Power semiconductor device figure of merit for high-frequency applications

Year: 1989

Primary subgenre: figure of merit

Secondary subgenres: powersilicon carbide semiconductor technologies

Summary: A figure of merit (the Baliga high-frequency figure of merit) is derived for power semiconductor devices operating in high-frequency circuits. Using this figure of merit, it is predicted that the power losses incurred in the power device will increase as the square root of the operating frequency and approximately in proportion to the output power.
