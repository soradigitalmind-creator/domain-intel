Topological quantization of the spin Hall effect in two-dimensional paramagnetic semiconductors

Year: 2006

Primary subgenre: magnetic semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: magnetic fieldparamagnetismspin

Summary: We propose models of two-dimensional paramagnetic semiconductors where the intrinsic spin Hall effect is exactly quantized in integer units of a topological charge. The model describes a topological insulator in the bulk and a ``holographic metal'' at the edge, where the number of extended edge states crossing the Fermi level is dictated by (exactly equal to) the bulk topological charge.
