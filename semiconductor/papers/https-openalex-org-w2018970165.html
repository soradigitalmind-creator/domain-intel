Band offsets and heterostructures of two-dimensional semiconductors

Year: 2013

Primary subgenre: wide-bandgap semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: band offset

Summary: The band offsets and heterostructures of monolayer and few-layer transition-metal dichalcogenides MX2 (M = Mo, W; X = S, Se, Te) are investigated from first principles calculations. The band alignments between different MX2 monolayers are calculated using the vacuum level as reference, and a simple model is proposed to explain the observed chemical trends.
