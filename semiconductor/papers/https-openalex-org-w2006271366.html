Band parameters for III–V compound semiconductors and their alloys

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: wide-bandgap semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: wurtzite crystal structurezinc

Summary: We present a comprehensive, up-to-date compilation of band parameters for the technologically important III–V zinc blende and wurtzite compound semiconductors: GaAs, GaSb, GaP, GaN, AlAs, AlSb, AlP, AlN, InAs, InSb, InP, and InN, along with their ternary and quaternary alloys.
