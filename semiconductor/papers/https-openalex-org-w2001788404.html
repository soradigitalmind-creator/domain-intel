Year: 1964

Primary subgenre: zno doping and property

Secondary subgenres: band gap

Summary: The position of the Fermi level at a metal-semiconductor interface relative to the conduction band has been found to be a constant fraction of the semiconductor band gap for all but 3 of the 14 group IV or III-V semiconductors studied.
