Hole-mediated ferromagnetism in tetrahedrally coordinated semiconductors

Year: 2001

Primary subgenre: magnetic semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: delocalized electronzno doping and propertyferromagnetism

Summary: A mean-field model of ferromagnetism mediated by delocalized or weakly localized holes in zinc-blende and wurzite diluted magnetic semiconductors is presented. The model takes into account strong spin-orbit and $k\ensuremath{\cdot}p$ couplings in the valence band as well as the influence of strain upon the hole density of states.
