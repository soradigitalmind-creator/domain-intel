Metal-semiconductor contacts

Year: 1982

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: A review is given of our present knowledge of metal-semiconductor contacts. Topics covered include the factors that determine the height of the Schottky barrier, its current/voltage characteristics, and its capacitance. A short discussion is also given of practical contacts and their application in semiconductor technology, and a comparison is made with p-n junctions.
