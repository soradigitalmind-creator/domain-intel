Overview of atomic layer etching in the semiconductor industry

Year: 2015

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: semiconductor industrysemiconductor material

Summary: Atomic layer etching (ALE) is a technique for removing thin layers of material using sequential reaction steps that are self-limiting. ALE has been studied in the laboratory for more than 25 years. Today, it is being driven by the semiconductor industry as an alternative to continuous etching and is viewed as an essential counterpart to atomic layer deposition.
