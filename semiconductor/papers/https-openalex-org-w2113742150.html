Year: 1970

Primary subgenre: compound semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: charge carriersemiconductor material

Summary: In this paper we describe a new semiconductor device concept. Basically, it consists of storing charge in potential wells created at the surface of a semiconductor and moving the charge (representing information) over the surface by moving the potential minima.
