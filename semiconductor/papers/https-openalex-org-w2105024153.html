Semiconductor Gas Sensors: Dry Synthesis and Application

Year: 2010

Primary subgenre: organic chemistry

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: Since the development of the first chemoresistive metal oxide based gas sensors, transducers with innovative properties have been prepared by a variety of wet- and dry-deposition methods. Among these, direct assembly of nanostructured films from the gas phase promises simple fabrication and control and with the appropriate synthesis and deposition methods nm to μm thick films, can be prepared.
