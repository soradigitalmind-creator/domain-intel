Thermodynamic Oxidation and Reduction Potentials of Photocatalytic Semiconductors in Aqueous Solution

Year: 2012

Primary subgenre: band diagram

Secondary subgenres: band gapchemical physicsorganic chemistrywide-bandgap semiconductor

Summary: An approach is introduced to calculate the thermodynamic oxidation and reduction potentials of semiconductors in aqueous solution. By combining a newly developed ab initio calculation method for compound formation energy and band alignment with electrochemistry experimental data, this approach can be used to predict the stability of almost any compound semiconductor in aqueous solution.
