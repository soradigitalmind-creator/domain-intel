Strong Coupling between Surface Plasmons and Excitons in an Organic Semiconductor

Year: 2004

Primary subgenre: surface plasmon polariton

Secondary subgenres: organic semiconductor

Summary: We report on the observation of a strong coupling between a surface plasmon and an exciton. Reflectometry experiments are performed on an organic semiconductor, namely, cyanide dye J aggregates, deposited on a silver film.
