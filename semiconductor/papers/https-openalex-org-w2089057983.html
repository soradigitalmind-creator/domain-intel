Study of diluted magnetic semiconductor: Co-doped ZnO

Year: 2002

Primary subgenre: magnetic semiconductor

Secondary subgenres: lattice constantmagnetic fieldmagnetization

Summary: We report on the high-temperature ferromagnetism in Co-doped ZnO films fabricated by the sol–gel method above 350 K.
