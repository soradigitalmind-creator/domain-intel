Degenerate four-wave mixing in semiconductor-doped glasses

Year: 1983

Primary subgenre:

Secondary subgenres:

Summary: We report degenerate four-wave mixing (DFWM) of visible radiation in borosilicate glasses doped with crystallites of the mixed semiconductor CdSxSe1−x. These semiconductor-doped glasses—available commercially in the form of colored glass filters—exhibit third-order nonlinearities of ~10−9–10−8 esu for DFWM with short (~10-nsec) laser pulses at various visible wavelengths.
