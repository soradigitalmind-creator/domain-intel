Si Nano-Photodiode with a Surface Plasmon Antenna

Year: 2005  DOI ↗

Main topic: wave antenna

Abstract
Nano-photodiodes with a subwavelength active area using the optical near-field enhanced by surface plasmon resonance are proposed. We fabricated a Si Schottky photodiode that consists of an active area of 300 nm in diameter and a surface plasmon antenna to generate the carrier within the active area efficiently.
