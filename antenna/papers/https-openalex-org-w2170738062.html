Fading correlation and its effect on the capacity of multielement antenna systems

Year: 2000  DOI ↗

Main topic: antenna system

Abstract
We investigate the effects of fading correlations in multielement antenna (MEA) communication systems. Pioneering studies showed that if the fades connecting pairs of transmit and receive antenna elements are independently, identically distributed, MEAs offer a large increase in capacity compared to single-antenna systems.
