A Novel Method for Improving Antipodal Vivaldi Antenna Performance

Year: 2015  DOI ↗

Main topic: antenna design

Abstract
Herein, a new method for improving the directivity and bandwidth of the antipodal Vivaldi antenna structure is presented. The method is based on introducing a parasitic elliptical patch in the flare aperture to enhance the field coupling between the arms and produce stronger radiation in the endfire direction.
