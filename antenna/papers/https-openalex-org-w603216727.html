From the Publisher: The recent explosion in commercial applications involving RF and microwave systems (e.g. mobile telephones) is fueling customer demand for small, low-cost, easy-to-use systems. It's also driving innovations in antenna technology. This how-to book explains why microstrip antennas are the ideal low-profile, low-cost solution for these commercial applications and provides the information and tools necessary to design them.
