Limiting performance of block-fading channels with multiple antennas

Year: 2001  DOI ↗

Main topic: antenna diversity

Abstract
We derive the performance limits of a radio system consisting of a transmitter with t antennas and a receiver with r antennas, a block-fading channel with additive white Gaussian noise (AWGN), delay and transmit-power constraints, and perfect channel-state information available at both the transmitter and the receiver.
