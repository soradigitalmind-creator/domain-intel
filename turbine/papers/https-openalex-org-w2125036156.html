Large eddy simulations on the flow driven by a Rushton turbine

Year: 1999  DOI ↗

Main topic: —

Abstract
Abstract Large eddy simulations were performed on the flow in a baffled stirred tank, driven by a Rushton turbine at Re = 29,000. The simulation procedure consisted of a lattice–Boltzmann scheme for discretizing the Navier–Stokes equations, and a force‐field technique for representing the action of the impeller on the fluid. The subgrid‐scale model was a conventional Smagorinsky model with a Smagorinsky constant c s = 0.12. The uniform, cubic computational grid had a size of about 6 × 10 6 nodes
