Experimental and Numerical Studies for a High Head Francis Turbine at Several Operating Points

Year: 2013  DOI ↗

Main topic: turbine blade

Abstract
Experimental and numerical studies on a high head model Francis turbine were carried out over the entire range of turbine operation. A complete Hill diagram was constructed and pressure-time measurements were performed at several operating conditions over the entire range of power generation by installing pressure sensors in the rotating and stationary domains of the turbine.
