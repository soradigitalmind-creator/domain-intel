Plasma Actuators for Separation Control of Low-Pressure Turbine Blades

Year: 2006  DOI ↗

Main topic: turbine blade

Abstract
This work involves the documentation and control of flow separation that occurs over turbine blades in the low-pressure-turbine (LPT) stage at the low Reynolds numbers typical of high-altitude cruise. We utilize a specially constructed linear cascade that is designed to study the flowfield over a generic LPT cascade consisting of Pratt and Whitney Pak B shaped blades.
