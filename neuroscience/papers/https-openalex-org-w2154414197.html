OLFACTORY MEMORY FORMATION IN<i>DROSOPHILA</i>: From Molecular to Systems Neuroscience

Year: 2005  DOI ↗

Main topic: systems neuroscience

Abstract
The olfactory nervous system of insects and mammals exhibits many similarities, which suggests that the mechanisms for olfactory learning may be shared. Molecular genetic investigations of Drosophila learning have uncovered numerous genes whose gene products are essential for olfactory memory formation.
