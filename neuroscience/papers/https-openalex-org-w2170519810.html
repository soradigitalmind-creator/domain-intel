iCub: the design and realization of an open humanoid platform for cognitive and neuroscience research

Year: 2007  DOI ↗

Main topic: neuroscience research

Abstract
The development of robotic cognition and the advancement of understanding of human cognition form two of the current greatest challenges in robotics and neuroscience, respectively. The RobotCub project aims to develop an embodied robotic child (iCub) with the physical (height 90 cm and mass less than 23 kg) and ultimately cognitive abilities of a 2.5-year-old human child.
